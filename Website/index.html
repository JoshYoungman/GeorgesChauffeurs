Silverstone in Northhamptonshire is most famously home to the Silverstone racing circuit and home of the F1 British Grand Prix.

Following the Second World War, Silverstone circuit was created to make use of one of the now unneeded airfields and helped to reinstate international motor racing in Britain. Due to this, Silverstone makes for the perfect day out.

Why not treat some of your clients to an outing at the many racing events held at the Silverstone circuit. The Chelsea Flower Show in chauffeur style - The Chelsea Flower Show is one of the most famous flower shows anywhere in the world, and you can visit it in all its glory with chauffeur and lucury cars from GC Airport Transfers. Held each May in the grounds of the Royal Hospital at CHelsea in London, the CHelsea Flower Show showcases the latest trends in plants and gardening and features innovative designs from leading gardeners. The Royal Hospital Chelsea is home to retured soldiers known as Chelsea Pensioners, who dress in a distinctive scarlet uniform.

The preview of the show is visited by members of the Royal Family, making it one of the key events in London's social calender.

You do habe the option of travelling to the Chelsea Flower Show using punlic transport, but the huge number of visitors to the show annually - more than 150,000 people - puts a strain on local bus, tube and rail services, so take the stress out of getting to and from the show with an executive car and driver. We plan your journey using the best route and take traffic delays into account, so you get to your destination on time.
